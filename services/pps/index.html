Pay Progression Strategy

A structured, data-driven approach to annual raises that balances performance, equity, and market competitiveness. This service combines benchmarking, strategic design, and employee-level modeling to deliver a clear, defensible raise framework that supports retention, strengthens internal fairness, and aligns compensation decisions with your organization's goals.

How it works:

  • Compare your organization's salaries to market benchmarks to ground the raise strategy in real data
  • Explore multiple raise structure options
  • Facilitate decision-making with your leadership team
  • Build the customized raise model and calculate employee-leve raise outcomes


What you recieve:

  • Interactive Excel model with raise calculations, performance tiers, and scenario modeling functionality.
  • PDF summary report outlining project scope, methodology, assumptions, and recommended next steps for implementation.
