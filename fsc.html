Full Spectrum Compensation

A comprehensive, end-to-end compensation solution combining market alignment, performance pay design, and a data-driven pay progression strategy. This service brings all three components together into a single, integrated strategy - built to reward performance, strengthen retention, and keep pay aligned with your organization's goals and the market.

How it works:

  • Compare your organization's salaries to market benchmarks to ground the compensation in real data
  • Explore multiple raise and bonus structure options
  • Facilitate decision-making with your leadership team
  • Build the customized raise and bonus model and calculate employee-level raise and bonus outcomes


What you recieve:

  • Interactive Excel model with the finalized raise and bonus design, employee-level calculations, and scenario modeling functionality.
  • PDF summary report outlining project scope, methodology, assumptions, and recommended next steps for implementation.
